한국어 Vault

Korean vocabulary & grammar notes, mapped as an Obsidian-style graph.

Demo data. Currently showing a sample vault export. Drag nodes, scroll to zoom, drag the background to pan, click a note to open it. To go live: export the vocab vault with Webpage HTML Export, drop it in vault/, run python3 tools/build-graph.py vault.
